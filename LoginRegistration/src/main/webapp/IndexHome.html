ABOUT US

The team behind CardByte come from an enterprise background. During their previous stints in the industry, they realised how unorganized the professional sector is when it comes to contact storage and management. With the digital boom in the late 2000s and India alone accounting for 600 million smartphone users, it is hard to believe that paper business cards remain the prevalent method for professionals to network and exchange contact information with their peers. ‍ CardByte is India’s 1st end-to-end contact management & networking app that helps you store contacts & network seamlessly. Our vision is to transform how the new-age professional networks.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I share my digital business card?

The easiest way to share your digital business card is by getting your prospects to scan the digital business card in your Blinq Mobile app. You can also share your card by text or email if you prefer.

Is there a limit to how many scans I can have?

There isn’t! It's free, premium and business users have unlimited scans.

How do I get started with Digit Card?

Login to the web application , where you can create your card in few minutes.

How do I share my card?

Create your card in few minutes and ask for produce a QR code so that your card can be shared via QR code or via a link.

While sharing your details, is it necessary for the other person to have an account in Digit Card?

No, there is no need for the other user to have an account for seeing your card.
